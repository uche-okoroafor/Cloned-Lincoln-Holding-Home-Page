Our Venues

WELCOME TO

Lincoln Holding

Multi-award-winning hospitality group, Lincoln Holding is independently owned company comprising of a collective of expert restaurant consultants and business incubators. Helmed by Ralph Homer, CEO and Founder of Lincoln Holding, and Co-Founder of Restaurant Secrets Inc, it owns and operates Food & Beverage brands across the Middle East and Europe, including La Serre, Distillery and Taikun.

La Serre

BOULANGERIE

From 7am until late into the night the our Boulangerie hums to the tune of the eclectic mix of people that are the very fabric of Dubai, the ultimate hangout from Breakfast, through lunch right up to dinner. The Boulangerie is the meeting place, the hub that brings the Downtown community together.

La Serre

NIGHT

The jewel in the crown of La Serre, overlooking the stunning Boulevard below. The chic interior is matched with the ultimate French fine dining offering from Lunch through to Dinner. From the A la Carte to the decadent Degustation menu there is an experience that will take you on a culinary journey like no other

Taikun

RESTAURANT AND LOUNGE

Welcome to Taikun, where the flavors of East and West meet in a delectable combination of style, presentation and theatre. Located in the shadow of the iconic Burj our luxury lounge is the place to dine, dance and party the night away. With a world class entertainment programme our legendary Taikun performers will take you on adventure of the senses, this is no ordinary night out. This is Taikun.

Distillery

RESTOBAR

Distillery Dubai brings all the elements of what a great bar is. Drawing inspiration from around the globe we have created a social hub that brings you a truly traditional and hearty food menu, a world class beer selection not to mention our exceptional wine and spirits list If its cocktails you are after we have you covered, our team of award winning mixologists can cater to your every whim!
